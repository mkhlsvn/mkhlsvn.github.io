MELFYN CBS in details:

KYC – know your customers

MELFYN allows flexibly and extensively customize your clients' profiles. You set up which data fields you want to fill in when registering your organization's clients. You can register not only individual clients, but also corporate ones, and create groups. You also may define data fields when registering loan applications.

Loan module

Fully automated way to manage loans. Specify a loan product, enter the amount and interest rate and the system will automatically build a payment schedule and control the acceptance of payments through various channels. Is the loan overdue? The system will calculate the penalty. Want to restructure the loan or to use credit lines? The system calculates everything automatically.

Savings module

Allows to register your customers' accounts and receive deposits from them. MELFYN has both simple savings accounts and accounts with interest accrual, even with capitalization and further accrual on the changing account balance.

Term deposits module

Do you want to accept term deposits? MELFYN has a module for that as well. Set the term, the fees and the system will manage such deposits.

Accounting module

Automatically creates transactions in the system. You can control your accounting in MELFYN or export data to the accounting system you are used to.

Notifications module

Always keep you informed. Is a loan overdue? MELFYN will inform you via e-mail or SMS. Forgotten to disburse some loan? Our system will remind you.

Integration module

MELFYN contains a wide range of integration functions with third-party systems. You can integrate with payment terminals, credit bureaus, other banks, blacklists and even reporting systems for regulators.

Reporting module

Give you an extensive list of reports in which you will find all the required indicators. If you need a special report that is not included in the standard reports, you can create a template of the report you need in the report builder and then easily get the desired data.

Our mission

A lot of credit institutions in the world cannot afford modern core banking solution for their operational process due to high cost. These organizations suffer from the lack of automation, complexity of reporting and operational accounting.

Our initiative is to provide cloud solution for financial sector, which will be very easy to use, flexible in configuration and meet most of the requirements.

We gathered a team of professionals from different industries such as Fintech, Cybersecurity, Microfinance and Banking and set ourselves the goal to develop the best and most user friendly information system.

We don't see companies that decided to use our solution as clients, we see them as our partners. We try to listen to their needs as carefully as possible and provide solutions that meet their needs the best.

We invite everyone who is interested to join our friendly team and help with their knowledge and work to create secure, fast, simple and clear banking solution.
